# Aethera Life

Aethera Life is a consumer wellness brand platform designed to support supplement discovery, product education, and digital customer engagement.

## Problem

Consumers often struggle to understand which wellness products fit their goals, routines, and lifestyle.

Aethera Life focuses on creating a cleaner, more guided product experience for supplement discovery and customer education.

## Solution

The platform combines product information, brand storytelling, digital workflows, and AI personalization concepts to help users better understand wellness products and choose options aligned with their goals.

## Key Features

- Product discovery experience
- Supplement education flows
- Mobile app promotion and app-store linking
- Brand-focused landing pages
- Customer engagement workflows
- Direct-to-consumer product positioning
- AI personalization roadmap

## AI / LLM Relevance

This project demonstrates how LLMs can improve wellness product discovery through:

- Personalized product recommendation flows
- Natural-language supplement education
- Customer onboarding and preference capture
- FAQ automation
- AI-powered product guidance
- Content generation for marketing and education
